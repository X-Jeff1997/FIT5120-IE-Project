The data collected by Monash University Accident Research centre in 2020 suggests that 89.9% of vehicles failed to meet the safety system criteria for all types of crashes. Aside from poor driving skills and road conditions, mechanical failure, poor vehicle maintenance and tyre failure/condition is a major contributing factor to road accidents. Inexperienced drivers are the most vulnerable due to the lack of mechanical knowledge. Having a safe vehicle not only reduces your chance of getting into an accident, but also helps you to stay safe during an accident.
